The value of asking questions: Reina Reyes at TEDxDiliman

This is a talk of Reinabelle Reyes, a 29-year old astrophysicist by then, who astounded scientists all over the world when she led a Princeton University research team in 2010 to prove how galaxies up to 3.5 billion light years away are clustered together, in exactly the way that Einstein's General Relativity predicts.

One of my favorite TEDx Diliman talks from one of my favorite and greatly admired Filipino scientists.

In this talk about maps, historian and storyteller Ambeth Ocampo offers insights and reflections on our past, what it means to us in the present, and how we can meaningfully move forward as a people and as a nation.

This talk was delivered on June 30, 2019, as part of CANVAS'(I am part of CANVAS since 2013.) preparations to commemorate the 500th Anniversary of the first circumnavigation of the world in 2021.
